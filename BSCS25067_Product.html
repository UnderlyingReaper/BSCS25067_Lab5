Players step into the role of Aloy, a skilled hunter and archer, as she unravels the mysteries behind towering, animal-like machines that dominate the land. With its breathtaking landscapes, deep storytelling, and tactical combat, Horizon Zero Dawn delivers a unique blend of sci-fi and primal adventure that continues to captivate players worldwide.
